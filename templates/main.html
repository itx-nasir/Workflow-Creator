Welcome to Workflow Designer

Create and manage your workflows with ease.

How to Use

Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Create a new workflow by clicking on "Create New Workflow" in the User Dashboard.
  2. Add steps to your workflow by dragging and dropping them from the left sidebar.
  3. Arrange the steps in the desired order within the workflow container.
  4. Submit the workflow for approval.
  5. Manage your workflows and track their status in the User Dashboard.
